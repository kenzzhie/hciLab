    Mondstat

    Mondstadt is one of the seven regions in Teyvat, and the first in which the Traveler starts to look for their lost sibling. It is the city-state that worships Lord Barbatos, the Anemo Archon.

    Ever since the beginning, Mondstadt has celebrated a festival called the Ludi Harpastum every year, with 15 days of games for the children to enjoy.Before completing Archon Quest Prologue: Act I - The Outlander Who Caught the Wind, only small parts of Starfell Valley, Galesong Hill, and Windwail Highland are accessible.

    Li Yue

    Liyue is one of the seven regions in Teyvat. It is the city-state that worships Rex Lapis, the Geo Archon. The nation of Liyue is marked by its emphasis on "contracts," Morax's ideal. It is said that people from all seven nations of Teyvat flock to Liyue Harbor, the economic hub of the continent.

    Access to Liyue is unlocked after completing the Archon Quest Prologue: Act I - The Outlander Who Caught the Wind.

    Inazuma

    Inazuma is one of the seven regions in Teyvat. It is an archipelagic region that worships Raiden Shogun, the Electro Archon, who is also the leader of its governing body, the Inazuma Shogunate. Inazuma is located approximately 4 km southeast from Liyue Harbor and is a close neighbor with the Dark Sea.

    Access into Inazuma is unlocked after reaching Adventure Rank 30 and completing the first part of the Archon Quest Chapter II: Act I - The Immovable God and the Eternal Euthymia.

    Sumeru

    Sumeru is one of the seven regions in Teyvat. It is the nation that worships Lesser Lord Kusanali, the Dendro Archon.It is renowned as the center of learning and houses the Sumeru Akademiya. People in Sumeru value rationality and wisdom, and the fact that many adults in Sumeru do not have dreams is in fact a source of pride for them.

    Access to Sumeru is unlocked after completing the Archon Quest Prologue: Act I - The Outlander Who Caught the Wind.
